## Recommended Further Reading

### Books
- Barrett, L. F. (2017). *How Emotions Are Made.* — The most important single book for LEM's philosophical grounding.
- Damasio, A. (1994). *Descartes' Error.* — Why emotion is necessary for rational decision-making.
- Picard, R. (1997). *Affective Computing.* — The field's founding text.
- Minsky, M. (2006). *The Emotion Machine.* — AI pioneer on emotion as computation.
- Plutchik, R. (2003). *Emotions and Life.* — Comprehensive overview of emotion theory.

### Papers for Architecture Design
- Marsella & Gratch (2009) — EMA is the best existing computational appraisal model
- Graves et al. (2016) — DNC architecture for memory-augmented systems
- Gu & Dao (2023) — Mamba for long-sequence state modeling
- Chen et al. (2018) — Neural ODEs for continuous dynamics

### Papers for Philosophical Grounding
- Barrett (2016) — The theory of constructed emotion (PMC open access)
- Li et al. (2023) — Proof that LLMs can learn emotion concepts from language
- Seth (2013) — Interoceptive inference connects prediction to emotion
- Friston (2010) — Free energy principle as unified theory of brain function
